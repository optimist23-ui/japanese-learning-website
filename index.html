Japanese society and culture

Week 4 May 5 upload: family, ability, and Golden Week

Narrative

From classroom practice to cultural context

The class moved from warm conversation into survival-skill sentences using できます and できません, then into the particle も for “also.” The second half shifted toward family language and Japanese society: how family terms change when speaking about your own family versus someone else’s, and how Golden Week shapes travel, work, and everyday life in Japan.
